SETTING THE SCENE

Welcome to SKOMD! A short interactive story where your choices decide its end. The word ‘SKOMD’ has no real meaning, it is just a randomized jumble of words that I happened to have a positive opinion about on that time of day. I do believe that creating something in this way is a healthy practice and can bring a burning sense of pride in your work. Such a feeling can be experienced when painting on a blank canvas or building a quilt. This website is not about the material, but the process of its fabrication.

The story begins with you arriving home after completing all there is to do today. With no more obligations, you settle into your cozy slippers and enjoy your 48 oz. lime on the rocks margarita. As you go to take your first sip of tequila goodness you are, rather rudely might you say, interupted by something...
